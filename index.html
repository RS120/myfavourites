Soccer:

Soccer is a game played (usually) on an outdoor field. The competition is between two teams of 11 player. There are three diffent positions in soccer. These include:

  1. Defender
  2. Midfielder
  3. Forward

The object of the game is to propel a round ball into the opponent's goal by kicking or by hitting it with any part of the body except the hands and arms.
